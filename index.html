How EduAlat Works

Tired of teacher absenteeism, exam malpractice, harassment, or unsafe school environments? With EduAlat, you can report these issues anonymously and directly to the authorities.

1

Submit Anonymously

Report issues completely anonymously via our website or SMS - no registration needed.

2

Direct to Authorities

Your report goes straight to the relevant school authorities for immediate action.

3

Track Progress

Follow your report's progress with a unique tracking code while staying anonymous.

4

Drive Change

See real improvements in your school as issues are addressed and resolved.

Why Your Voice Matters

For too long, problems in schools have gone unreported due to fear or lack of accessible channels. EduAlat empowers students, parents, and teachers to create safer, more accountable learning environments without risking their safety.

Every report contributes to building a better education system where everyone can thrive in a safe and supportive environment.
